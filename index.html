Catholic Abolition Movement

Justice for the Unborn

A movement of Catholics working to establish equal protection for every human being from the moment of conception.

Our Mission

We believe that every human being is created in the image of God and possesses inherent dignity from the moment of conception.

Our mission is to work for the complete abolition of abortion by proclaiming the truth, forming consciences, and advocating for equal protection under the law.

What We Stand For

Equal Dignity

Every human being, from conception, possesses equal worth and value.

Equal Protection

We advocate for laws that recognize and protect unborn children as persons.

Faithful Witness

Our work is rooted in the teachings of the Catholic Church and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Justice & Mercy

We seek justice for the unborn while extending compassion and support to mothers and families.
